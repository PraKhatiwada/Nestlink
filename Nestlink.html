Welcome to Nestlink

Nestlink is a community-driven project aimed at supporting homeless individuals in the Hurstville area by connecting them with local shelters, food, and essential services. Our mission is to empower homeless communities by providing access to vital resources and fostering collaborations with local volunteers, healthcare providers, and businesses.

In Hurstville alone, nearly 260 people are currently without shelter. Nestlink seeks to make a tangible difference by offering a platform that allows easy access to shelters, food, medical care, and job opportunities for the homeless population.

Available Services

Our platform provides essential services that help homeless individuals rebuild their lives. We offer the following services through our network of local partners:

  • Safe and Secure Shelters
  • Access to Food Banks and Meals
  • Medical Care and Mental Health Support
  • Job Search Assistance and Employment Opportunities

These services are designed to meet the basic needs of the homeless community while helping them move toward long-term stability and self-sufficiency.

How to Get Involved

We welcome volunteers, donors, and local organizations to join us in making a difference. You can get involved in multiple ways:

  • Volunteer to help distribute food, clothing, and other essentials.
  • Donate to support our mission of helping homeless individuals.
  • Partner with us as a local shelter, healthcare provider, or business to expand our services.
